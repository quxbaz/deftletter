Make your cards

We cut our cards to the standard size which is 3.5 by 2 inches so your image file should have a width toheight ratio of 7:4. To have excellent printed image quality, we strongly suggest that your image contains at least 300 DPI. This means that the minimum pixel resolution of your image should be 1050x750 pixels. If you have any questions about this, you can contact us at card@card.com.

Oh no! Something's wrong with your image!

  • Your image resolution is too low. This could result in a blurry print. Please upload an image with a resolution of at least 1050x750 pixels.
  • Your image does not have the correct aspect ratio. You image will be partially cut off as a result. The width to height ratio should by 7:4. An image with the correct aspect ratio will occupy the entire space in the grid.

While you don't need to follow all of these suggestions, we highly suggest you do so that you obtain the best prints possible.
