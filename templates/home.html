Our cards

A card is only as good as the paper it's printed on. We at DeftLetter understand the importance of using quality materials. We've selected a paper stock at a mindful intersection between price and quality.

Our paper has the delicate texture of a newly hatched dinosaur egg and the sturdiness of a battle-tested goose feather.

We print in full vibrant color and beautiful stark black.

All of our cards are the standard size, 3.5 by 2 inches.
